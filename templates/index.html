About Me

Meet Shreya

"Every nail is a canvas, every client is my muse. I don't just do nails — I create art."

Hi! I'm Shreya, a passionate nail artist based in Kothrud, Pune. With years of hands-on experience and a love for all things beautiful, I specialize in creating stunning nail extensions and intricate nail art that perfectly complements your style.

From classic elegance to bold, experimental designs — I've got you covered. Every set is crafted with premium products and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your nails look flawless and last long.
